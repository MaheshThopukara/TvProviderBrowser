How to use exported assets

  1. Download each asset below
  2. Play Store Icon (512×512) → Upload to Google Play Console under "Store listing" → "App icon"
  3. TV Banner → Save as tv_banner.png in app/src/main/res/drawable-xhdpi/ (640×360) to replace the vector fallback
  4. Feature Graphic (1024×500) → Upload to Google Play Console under "Store listing" → "Feature graphic"
  5. TV Banner HD (1280×720) → Upload to Google Play Console under "TV" → "TV banner"
