Google TV / Android TV Compliance Checklist

  • Banner: 320x180 dp (export at xhdpi = 640x360 px) — fully opaque, no transparency
  • App name prominently displayed on banner — readable from 10-foot viewing distance
  • No emoji — replaced with vector/CSS shapes for consistent rendering across all TV devices
  • 10-foot UI text sizing — tagline at 14sp min, app name at 22sp+ on banner
  • Minimal clutter — max 2 visual elements (icon graphic + text) per banner
  • High contrast — text/background contrast ratio at least 4.5:1 (WCAG AA)
  • Adaptive icon safe zone — key artwork within 66% center circle (shown as dashed ring)
  • Icon: 512x512 px for Play Store — works inside circle, squircle, and rounded-rect masks
  • Colors match app theme — purple-tinted dark surfaces, blue→teal logo gradient, 3-color accents
